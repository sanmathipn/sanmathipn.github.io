Setup sync
Connect Stride to Google Sheets so your data syncs across your phone and laptop automatically.
1
Open Google Sheets
Go to sheets.new to create a new blank spreadsheet. Name it Stride Data.
2
Open Apps Script
In your sheet, click Extensions → Apps Script. Delete any existing code, paste the full contents of google-apps-script.js (from your zip file), then click Save (💾).
3
Deploy as Web App
Click Deploy → New deployment. Set type to Web app. Set "Execute as" to Me. Set "Who has access" to Anyone. Click Deploy → copy the URL.
4
Paste your URL below
